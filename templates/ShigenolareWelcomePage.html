What is Trägen?

Trägen is a conlang invented by E. R. Minnix III when he was in his late teens. Its people are known as either the Tra, the Trahuli, or the Träpéndi (the name Trägen is derived from the name Tra and the word geno, meaning language). It is an agglutinative language with a morphology full of semantic affixes and compounds.

Šigënolare is the only place where one can find the information necessary for learning Trägen

Šigënolare (Trägen for place for the language teacher) is a website designed by the creator of Trägen himself in order to let others learn his language.

Trägen is a language with a grammar unlike that of English, and so can be kind of odd for English speakers to learn. Trägen is an agglutinative language, meaning it tends to "glue" things together in order to form new words. (e.g., šigënolare is derived from the roots ši-, place; geno, language; and lare, to teach, teacher, &c.)
